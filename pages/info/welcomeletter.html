Esteemed participants of BKLMUN'18,

My name is İrem AKÇAY and I am honoured to be hosting you as the Secretary General of the first annual session of BKLMUN. Our team and I will be welcoming you between 27th and 30th of September, 2018.

I strongly believe MUN conferences are one of the best experiences a high-schooler can have, since when discussing topics that effect the world we live in and trying to make our world a better place, planning for future and resolving current problems; we can have lots of fun, make friends and improve our skills of communication and public speaking.

It was not easy for us to gather and work as there were lots of difficulties in front of us. But I am proud to be a member of such a great team, that has worked a lot to make this conference come to life. You can make sure that BKLMUN’18 will be one of the best MUN conferences you have ever attended to, and I am certain that you will remember it as a magnificent experience.

We put a lot of effort to choose our committees and agenda items, and I hope every delegate will be satisfied with our academic part of the conference as well as the organization part. I cannot wait to welcome you in our conference in September and I hope you will enjoy BKLMUN’18.

Secretary General
İrem AKÇAY
