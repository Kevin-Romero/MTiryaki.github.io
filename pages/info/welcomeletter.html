Honorable Participants of 60MUN'18,

As the representative of the Executive Team I welcome you to the second annual session of 60MUN. My name is Ozan Uygun and I will accompany you as the Secretary General of 60MUN'18. I deeply live the honor and pride of declaring you as participants of this prestigious and qualified conference.

Last year 60MUN delivered a special conference to its participants with a hardworking and avant-garde team, creating a new tradition in İzmir. Serving this tradition as the Secretary General is indescribable for me. Since I am aware of this responsibility, me and my colleagues have worked for a great conference for our delegates' pleasure and 60MUN's quality construction.

This year's session of 60MUN will be held between 29 January – 1 February 2018 at Izmir Democracy University with its exciting and impressive committees. The Academic and Organization Teams have worked for a long time for your pleasure. As the biggest witness of the preperation process, I would like to say that every member of the executive teams have done their best for a great conference.

Me and my Academic Team have worked so hard to give you the best experience from our committees. We have worked meticulously on every single committee and agenda item and we guarantee you that every participant will enjoy their committee.

Me and my Executive Team are looking forward to see our delegates in their committees and work with them. So, I welcome you to 60MUN family and I hope you enjoy 60MUN'18.

Ozan Uygun
Secretary General
